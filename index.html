    Our Products

    Milo

    Milo is a humanoid robot designed to assist children with autism. Created by RoboKind, it helps improve communication, social skills, and emotional development through interactive activities. With expressive features and engaging content, Milo supports therapy by teaching social cues and language, boosting confidence and social engagement in children.

    Smart Bird

    SmartBird is a bio-inspired robotic bird developed by Festo. It mimics the flight mechanics of real birds, with wings that flap independently to achieve lifelike, energy-efficient flight. Designed for research and development, SmartBird showcases advancements in robotics, aerodynamics, and biomimicry, highlighting the potential for future engineering applications.

    Ameca

    Ameca is a highly advanced humanoid robot developed by Engineered Arts. Known for its lifelike facial expressions and realistic movements, Ameca is designed to interact with humans in a natural way. It showcases cutting-edge AI, robotics, and human-robot interaction, making it a key platform for developing future humanoid technologies.

    Types of Robots

    Types of robots include industrial, service, humanoid, autonomous, collaborative, medical, exploration, agricultural, military, and educational robots.

    Tools

    Tools for robot building include 3D printers, CAD software, CNC machines, motors, sensors, controllers, welding equipment, and circuit boards.

    Software

    Software used for robot building includes ROS, VEXcode, MATLAB, SolidWorks, Python, LabVIEW, Autodesk, Simulink, and RobotStudio.

    Hardware

    Hardware for building robots includes motors, sensors, actuators, controllers, batteries, microcontrollers, servos, frames, wiring, and circuit boards.
